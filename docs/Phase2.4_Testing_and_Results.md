* **Partition 1 (Row 6):** Column A is empty (`A:`).
* **Partition 2 (Rows 7+):** Column A begins its recursive increment (`A:+Col[A][-1]+1`).

### Anomaly 2: The Multi-Tier Mortality Shifts
The most critical discovery was found in **Column I** (*Cost of Insurance Rate Per 1000*). Instead of a single static shift at row 54, the actuary who built the spreadsheet hardcoded gradual step-up increments into the formulas across 5 separate age cohorts.
